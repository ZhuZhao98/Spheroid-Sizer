Spheroid Sizer-updated 250428

This tool automatically segments the spheroid using a threshold-based method (as a stand-in for active contour segmentation) and extracts key geometric properties. In addition, it offers a manual mode that allows users to draw custom masks—either as freehand brush strokes or closed contours—for direct measurement and comparison with the automated results.

  • Pixel Volume and Real Volume (mm³)
  • Pixel Area and Real Area (mm²) – computed using a calibration red bar
  • Major Axis Length
  • Minor Axis Length
  • Eccentricity
The calibration uses a red bar known to be 0.5 mm long.
